### **Poulet DG (Director’s Chicken)**

**Prep time:** 15 mins
**Cook time:** 35–40 mins
**Serves:** 4

**Ingredients:**

* 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces)
* 2 carrots (sliced)
* 2 plantains (sliced)
* 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
* 1 onion (sliced)
* 3 cloves garlic (crushed)
* 3 tbsp vegetable oil
* 1 tsp curry powder
* Salt & Maggi
* Black pepper

**Instructions:**

1. Marinate the chicken with garlic, curry powder, salt, and black pepper for 10 mins.
2. Fry the chicken in oil until golden brown, then remove and set aside.
3. Sauté onions, bell peppers, and carrots in the same pan for 5 mins.
4. Add the chicken back, mix, and cover for 10 mins.
5. Fry plantains separately until golden.
6. Serve chicken with plantains.
