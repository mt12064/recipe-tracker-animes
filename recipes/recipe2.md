Prep time: 20 min | Cook time: 45 min | Serves: 6

Ingredients

Rice, tomato paste, fresh tomatoes, onions, garlic, ginger, vegetable oil, stock, salt, seasoning cubes, bay leaf.

Steps

Blend tomatoes, onions, garlic and ginger.

Fry the mixture in oil until the tomato base reduces.

Add tomato paste and fry again.

Pour in stock, bay leaf, salt and seasoning.

Add the rice and cook on low heat until done.

Adjust seasoning and serve warm.
A West African classic.
