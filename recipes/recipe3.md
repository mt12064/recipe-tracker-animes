Prep time: 25 min | Cook time: 1 hour | Serves: 8

Ingredients

Koki beans, red palm oil, crayfish, salt, plantain leaves, pepper.

Steps

Peel and wash the koki beans.

Grind the beans into a smooth paste with pepper.

Mix in salt, crayfish and warm palm oil.

Wrap the mixture in plantain leaves.

Steam until firm.
A true village favorite.
