### **Cameroon Tomato Stew**

**Prep time:** 10 mins
**Cook time:** 25–30 mins
**Serves:** 4

**Ingredients:**

* 4 medium tomatoes (blended)
* 1 red bell pepper (blended)
* 1 onion (chopped)
* 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
* 1/4 cup vegetable oil
* 1 tsp tomato paste
* Salt & Maggi to taste
* 1/2 tsp thyme or curry powder
* Protein: chicken, beef, or fish (optional)

**Instructions:**

1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add blended tomatoes and bell pepper, and fry for 10 mins until reduced.
3. Stir in tomato paste, salt, Maggi, and spices.
4. Add protein if using, cover, and simmer for 15 mins.
5. Adjust seasoning and serve hot with rice, plantains, or fufu.
