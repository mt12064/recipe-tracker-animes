Prep time: 30 min | Cook time: 1 hour 15 min | Serves: 10

Ingredients

Eru leaves, waterleaf, beef, cow skin, smoked fish, crayfish, red oil, salt, seasoning cubes.

Steps

Boil beef and cow skin with seasoning.

Add smoked fish and crayfish.

Mix in the waterleaf and let it release moisture.

Add eru leaves and stir well.

Pour in palm oil and simmer until the pot thickens.
Perfect with garri or fufu.
