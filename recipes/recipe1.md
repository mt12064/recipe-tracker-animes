Prep time: 30 min | Cook time: 1 hour | Serves: 8

Ingredients

Washed bitter leaves, peanut paste, beef, crayfish, smoked fish, onions, garlic, salt, seasoning cubes, palm oil.

Steps

Boil the beef with onions, garlic, salt and seasoning.

Blend the peanut paste and cook it until it thickens.

Add the peanut mixture to the pot of beef and let it simmer.

Add crayfish and smoked fish.

Stir in the washed bitter leaves and add palm oil.

Let it cook on low heat until everything blends well.
