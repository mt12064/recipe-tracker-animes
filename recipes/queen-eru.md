Eru & Garri
Prep time: 10 mins
Cook time: 20–25 mins
Serves: 3

Ingredients:

2 handfuls eru (washed and chopped)

1 handful waterleaf (chopped)

1 cup palm oil

1 tbsp crayfish

Pepper to taste

2–3 Maggi cubes

Smoked fish or beef (optional)

Salt to taste

Garri for serving

Instructions:

Put the eru and waterleaf in a pot with a small amount of water and let it soften for a few minutes.

Add palm oil and stir until the greens are fully coated.

Mix in crayfish, pepper, Maggi, smoked fish or beef, and a little salt.

Allow it to cook on low heat until the oil comes out on top and the pot dries slightly.

Serve hot with soaked garri or fufu.
