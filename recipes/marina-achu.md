# Achu Soup (Yellow Soup)

**Ingredients:**
- 1kg cow skin, tripe, and beef
- 1 tsp limestone (kanwa)
- 1 cup palm oil
- 1 tsp achu spice mix
- Salt and Maggi to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Boil meats with salt and seasoning until tender.
2. Dissolve limestone in warm water and strain.
3. Mix palm oil with limestone water until it turns yellow.
4. Add achu spice and meat broth. Stir well.
5. Add meat and adjust seasoning.
6. Serve with pounded cocoyams (achu).
