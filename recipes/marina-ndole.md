# Ndolé (Bitterleaf Stew)

**Ingredients:**
- 500g bitterleaf (washed)
- 500g groundnuts (peanuts), roasted and blended
- 500g beef or smoked fish
- 1 cup crayfish
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 seasoning cubes
- Palm oil
- Salt to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Boil the meat or fish with salt and seasoning cubes until tender.
2. In a separate pot, cook the bitterleaf until soft and no longer bitter.
3. Heat palm oil, sauté onions and crayfish.
4. Add the groundnut paste and stir continuously to avoid burning.
5. Add the meat/fish and bitterleaf. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
6. Adjust seasoning and serve with plantains or rice.
