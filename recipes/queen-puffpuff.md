### **Puff Puff (Cameroon Snack)**

**Prep time:** 10 mins
**Cook time:** 15 mins
**Serves:** 4

**Ingredients:**

* 2 cups all-purpose flour
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 1 tsp yeast
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 1 cup warm water
* Oil for deep frying

**Instructions:**

1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let sit 5 mins.
2. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
3. Add yeast mixture and stir into a smooth batter.
4. Cover and let rise 30â€“45 mins until doubled.
5. Heat oil in a deep pan.
6. Drop batter by spoonfuls into hot oil and fry until golden brown.
7. Drain and serve warm.
