# Eru and Water Fufu

**Ingredients:**
- 500g eru leaves (or okok)
- 300g waterleaf or spinach
- 500g beef, cow skin, or tripe
- 1 cup crayfish
- 1 cup palm oil
- 2 seasoning cubes
- Salt to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Boil meats until soft. Set aside.
2. Cook waterleaf/spinach until water reduces.
3. Add eru and stir well.
4. Add palm oil, crayfish, meat, and seasoning.
5. Simmer for 15â€“20 minutes.
6. Serve with water fufu or garri.
