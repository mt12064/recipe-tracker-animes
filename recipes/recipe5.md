Prep time: 10 min | Cook time: 15 min | Serves: 2

Ingredients

Ripe plantains, eggs, onions, tomatoes, oil, salt, pepper.

Steps

Slice and fry plantains until golden.

Beat eggs and mix with chopped onions, tomatoes, salt and pepper.

Fry the egg mixture until firm.

Serve with the fried plantains.
