# Koki (Steamed Black-Eyed Pea Pudding)

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups black-eyed peas
- 1 cup red palm oil
- 1 tsp ground crayfish
- 1 tsp salt
- Banana leaves or foil for wrapping

**Instructions:**
1. Peel and blend the beans into a smooth paste.
2. Add salt, crayfish, and palm oil. Mix until fluffy.
3. Wrap in banana leaves or foil.

5. Serve with boiled plantains or yams.
