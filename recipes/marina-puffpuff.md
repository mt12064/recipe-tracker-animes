# Puff-Puff (Sweet Fried Dough)

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 tsp yeast
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup warm water
- Oil for frying

**Instructions:**
1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
2. Add warm water and mix into a smooth batter.
3. Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
4. Heat oil and drop batter in small balls.
5. Fry until golden brown.
6. Drain and serve warm.
