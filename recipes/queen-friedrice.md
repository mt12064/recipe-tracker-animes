### **Cameroon Fried Rice**

**Prep time:** 10 mins
**Cook time:** 25 mins
**Serves:** 4

**Ingredients:**

* 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
* 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, green beans)
* 1 small onion (chopped)
* 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
* 1/2 cup cooked shrimp, chicken, or beef (optional)
* 2–3 tbsp vegetable oil
* 1–2 tbsp soy sauce
* Salt & pepper to taste

**Instructions:**

1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add vegetables and protein (if using) and stir-fry for 3–5 mins.
3. Add the rice and mix well.
4. Pour in soy sauce, season with salt and pepper, and stir until evenly coated.
5. Fry for 5–7 mins, stirring occasionally.
6. Serve hot.
